Club Championship - Veterans

Seniors Index Women

All first claim veteran members of the club are eligible for this competition. To qualify, you must ride at least two non-ten TTs – e.g. 9.5 mile hard-ride, 25 mile TT, etc.
Your competition score is the total of the points from your 2 highest scoring non‑ten events, plus your best 9 other events of any distance.
